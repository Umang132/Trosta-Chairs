About Trosta Chairs

Trosta Chairs is a brand by Intact Seating System, a revolving chair manufacturing unit based in Rakhiyal, Ahmedabad, Gujarat. We specialize in crafting high-quality office seating solutions for corporates, startups, home offices, and institutions.

Our mission is simple — deliver premium quality revolving chairs at the best possible price by cutting out the middleman. Every chair is manufactured in our own facility, ensuring strict quality control from raw materials to the finished product.
